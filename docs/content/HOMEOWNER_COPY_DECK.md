## Safety disclaimer

HVAC Truth provides homeowner education and safe first-step guidance. It does not replace a licensed HVAC contractor, electrician, engineer, inspector, or code official. Do not open electrical compartments, bypass safety switches, handle refrigerant, modify wiring, or continue operating equipment that smells like burning, trips breakers, leaks badly, or appears unsafe.

## Quote checker disclaimer

Quote Checker gives a first-pass review based on repair type, scope, system age, urgency, and available pricing assumptions. It does not prove a contractor is right or wrong. Always ask for written scope, test readings, photos, part details, and warranty terms.

## Technician call script

Hi, my AC is not working correctly. I already checked the thermostat, filter, breaker, and visible airflow issues. I would like a diagnostic with test readings, photos if possible, and a written explanation before approving repairs.
